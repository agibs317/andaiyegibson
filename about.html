About Me

I’m and Atlanta-native with a B.Sc. in Interactive Media who crafts immersive digital environments that captivate and connect. I am fluent in both English and Spanish as well as a variety of coding languages such as Java, JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I have a love for UX Design and Front-End Web Development as I’m passionate about the intersection between creativity and technology, and using different medias to create unique experiences. Through my work as a UX Designer for the Virtual Experiences Simulation Lab (VESL) at the University of Miami, I was able to hone in on my skills further developed my cross team collaboration and efforts for client success. I hope to continue to create meaningful digital experiences that enrich people’s lives and learn new things.
